About Company

The start-up company was started in 2018 with the funding from R.M.K. Engineering College for training students in the area of Robotics and enabling them to develop project and expertise in Robotics. The company has been doing in-house projects for the college and also for outside clients with the objective of giving real life experience to students. It is planned to commercialize the projects developed as products and services to clients. Currently, in 2022 the star-up company plans to create another division in the area of VLSI and provide products and services to clients. The planning is in progress.

ROBOTICS

The start-up company utilized the facilities set up in R.M.K. Engineering College which has established an Embedded System and Robotics under the e-Yantra initiative. This was Initiated by IIT Bombay and Sponsored by MHRD under the National Mission on Education through ICT program.
